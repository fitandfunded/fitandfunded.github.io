Privacy Policy for Fit and Funded

Last updated: March 06, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Fit and Funded collects, uses, and shares information when you use our services.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from users. Our service is an automated content publishing tool that posts content to social media platforms on behalf of a single account owner. No user data, cookies, or tracking mechanisms are used.

How We Use Information

Since we do not collect personal information, we do not use or share any personal data. The service only interacts with TikTok and Instagram APIs to publish pre-generated content.

Third Party Services

Our service integrates with the following third party platforms:

  • TikTok — governed by TikTok's own Privacy Policy
  • Instagram — governed by Meta's Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Claude API — used for content generation

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third party services.

Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect any information associated with our service. API credentials and access tokens are stored locally and are never shared with third parties beyond the platforms they are intended for.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
